Hachi Team – StartUp Lab

Project Overview

Leveraging augmented reality to enhance educational engagement as part of CLab Trento, in collaboration with HIT (Hub Innovazione Trentino).

From Problem to Team

A quick overview of challenge, solution and go-to-market, in one glance.

The Problem

Traditional teaching methods are often limited in engaging students and can result in reduced learning outcomes. Hachi addresses this issue by incorporating augmented reality (AR) to create interactive, immersive learning experiences.

Our Solution

Hachi’s platform allows educators to create AR-enhanced lessons with interactive 3D models. These models can be visualized via printable cards, making complex subjects more accessible and engaging for students.

Competitive Advantage

Hachi stands out for its affordability, customizability, and ease of use. Unlike other AR tools, our platform enables educators to collaborate with designers for a tailored educational experience.

Market Analysis

The demand for innovative educational tools is growing, particularly in the EdTech sector. Hachi fills this gap with an AR solution specifically designed to enhance classroom engagement.

Business Model

We operate on a two-sided platform model, connecting educators with AR designers to create custom content. This approach provides value for both, offering educators unique learning experiences and designers a platform for visibility.

Roadmap

Our initial goal is to launch AR content kits for schools in the Trentino region. We plan to expand by onboarding additional designers, allowing for diverse content and personalized educational experiences.

Meet the Team

Our team combines expertise in Flutter, AR development, product design, and business analysis, providing the technical and management skills needed for successful project execution.

Technical Details

Here’s a quick look under the hood—showcasing key technologies and components that make Hachi work.

  • Framework: Flutter per lo sviluppo mobile cross-platform.
  • AR Toolkit: Pacchetti Flutter AR-ready per tracking e rendering.
  • 3D Assets: Modelli 3D renderizzati sopra card stampabili.
  • Cross-Platform: Compatibile con dispositivi iOS e Android.
